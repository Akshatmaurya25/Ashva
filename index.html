Engineer

An engineer is a professional who uses science, math, and technical knowledge to design, create, and maintain systems, structures, and processes, solving problems and driving technological progress.

Doctor

A doctor is a highly trained medical professional who diagnoses, treats, and cares for patients' physical and mental health conditions, using their expertise to promote well-being and save lives.

Lawyer

A lawyer is a legal professional who provides advice, represents clients in legal matters, and advocates for their rights and interests in various areas of law, such as civil, criminal, or corporate law.

Charted Accountant

A Chartered Accountant (CA) is a financial professional who possesses expertise in auditing, accounting, and taxation. They provide financial advice, ensure compliance with regulations, and help organizations manage their finances effectively.
